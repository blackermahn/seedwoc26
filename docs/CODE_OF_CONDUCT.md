# CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md

## Our Commitment

SeedWOC is committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We believe in fostering an inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and respected.

## Our Values

### Inclusivity
We welcome people of all backgrounds, experience levels, and perspectives. No matter where you're from or your background, you belong here.

### Respect
We treat each other with kindness and respect. Disagreements are opportunities to learn, not battles to be won.

### Growth Mindset
We believe everyone can learn and grow. We support each other's learning journey without judgment.

### Collaboration
We succeed together. We help each other, share knowledge freely, and celebrate each other's successes.

## Expected Behavior

### Do This:
✅ Be respectful and professional in all interactions
✅ Welcome newcomers and help them get started
✅ Provide constructive feedback with kindness
✅ Share knowledge and help others learn
✅ Give credit to others for their ideas
✅ Admit when you're wrong and learn from it
✅ Focus on what's best for the community
✅ Use inclusive language
✅ Listen actively to others
✅ Celebrate diversity

### Don't Do This:
❌ Harassment or discrimination of any kind
❌ Insulting or demeaning comments
❌ Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
❌ Threats or intimidating behavior
❌ Spamming or trolling
❌ Plagiarism or taking credit for others' work
❌ Excluding people based on who they are
❌ Publishing others' private information
❌ Off-topic derailing of discussions
❌ Dismissing people's experiences or concerns

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

1. **Harassment**: Any unwanted conduct that creates an intimidating, hostile, or offensive environment
2. **Discrimination**: Treating someone unfairly based on protected characteristics
3. **Bullying**: Repeated harmful behavior intended to demean or intimidate
4. **Hate Speech**: Language that attacks or dehumanizes people based on identity
5. **Privacy Violations**: Sharing someone's personal information without consent
6. **Threats**: Any threatening language or behavior
7. **Self-promotion spam**: Excessive promotion of your own products/services

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub issues and discussions
- Pull request comments
- Community chat (Discord, Slack, etc.)
- Project events and meetings
- Interactions between community members
- Anything affiliated with the SeedWOC project

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document it**: Note what happened, when, and who was involved
2. **Report privately**: Email [conduct@seedwoc.org](mailto:conduct@seedwoc.org)
3. **Provide context**: Include as much detail as comfortable
4. **Stay safe**: If you feel unsafe, prioritize your safety first

### What Happens Next:
- Your report will be handled confidentially
- Investigation will start within 48 hours
- You won't be penalized for reporting in good faith
- Follow-up support will be offered

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

### Consequences for Violations:

1. **Warning**: For minor, first-time violations
2. **Temporary Mute**: Restricted commenting (1-7 days)
3. **Temporary Ban**: Temporary removal from community (1-30 days)
4. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal for severe or repeated violations

### Appeal Process:
If you disagree with an enforcement decision, you can appeal within 7 days. Appeals are handled by a different team member.

## Our Responsibilities

Community leaders will:
- Clarify standards of acceptable behavior
- Take appropriate action for unacceptable behavior
- Provide fair and impartial responses
- Protect the privacy of reporters
- Be transparent about decisions
- Act in good faith

## Support Resources

If you need help:
- **Mental Health**: [CAMS: Crisis Support](https://www.crisistextline.org/)
- **Safety**: Contact local authorities if in immediate danger
- **Mentorship**: Request mentor through community
- **Technical Help**: Ask in forums/discussions

## Contributors' Pledge

As contributors and maintainers of SeedWOC, we pledge to:

1. Make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:
   - Age
   - Body size
   - Caste
   - Disability
   - Ethnicity
   - Gender identity and expression
   - Experience level
   - Immigration status
   - Nationality
   - Neurodiversity
   - Personal appearance
   - Religion
   - Sex, including reproductive autonomy
   - Sexual orientation
   - Socioeconomic status

2. Act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, and healthy community

3. Call out unacceptable behavior and hold ourselves and others accountable
